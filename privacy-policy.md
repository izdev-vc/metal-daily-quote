# Privacy Policy for Metal Daily Quote

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Metal Daily Quote ("the App") is developed by izdev-vc.

## Information We Collect
The App does not collect any personal information from users.
The App does not require registration or login.
The App does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

## Data Storage
All quotes are stored in a secure cloud database (Supabase).
No user data is stored on our servers.

## Third Party Services
The App uses Supabase to retrieve daily quotes.
Supabase privacy policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

## Children's Privacy
The App contains lyrics that may not be suitable for children 
under 17. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
